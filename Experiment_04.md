## 7ï¸âƒ£ Display name, sal, hra, da, pf and total salary for each employee.

- HRA = 15% of sal  
- DA = 10% of sal  
- PF = 5% of sal  
- Total Salary = (sal + hra + da) - pf  

```sql
SELECT ename,
       sal,
       sal * 0.15 AS hra,
       sal * 0.10 AS da,
       sal * 0.05 AS pf,
       (sal + (sal * 0.15) + (sal * 0.10)) - (sal * 0.05) AS total_salary
FROM employee;
```
